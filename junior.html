HIST 113: This was a great class. I thought I had learned enough about the American Revolution and The Civil War in high school but I was very wrong. In this class we went much deeper and I learned a lot of new things and had a good time.

MATH 476: Certainly one of the hardest classes I have taken in my life. The content was super interesting but the homework was tough. I learned a lot about my ability to stay persistent when things get tough. I also learned just how great of a resource the Professor can be during office hours to help me out. In the end I got through the class and I am glad I took it. With this class done I am close to finishing up the math major.

CS 203X: This class was a pass/fail only class that was basically like being in a club. We had a Professor oversee us as we spent the first few classes preparing to compete in the Nation Cyber League (NCL). The main part of the competition was the Fall Regular Season.
