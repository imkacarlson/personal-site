Fall 2015

2015 Northwest Conference Cross Country Championship
We were the 2015 Northwest Conference Cross Country Team Champions! I finished 10th overall in the race and earned 2nd Team All-Conference Honors.
 
2015 NCAA Division III Cross Country Championships
No better way to end the season then a trip to nationals! Both the guys and girls teams qualified and I was so grateful to get this experience as a freshman. The men's team finished 28th in the nation and the women 10th!

Spring 2017

It was great to go into this season knowing what to expect being my second track season. I continued with my events from last year running the 3,000 meter steeplechase and 5k. I was fortunate to make the travel team and going to San Francisco and Chicago were some of the highlights of the season. I set new Personal Records in both the steeple and 5k and improved upon my finishes from last year at conference meet.
Photo: Christopher Oertell

Fall 2017

I had my best ever summer of training before the season and the hard work paid off! Unlike the past two years I ran really strong on the postseason races this year. I took 2nd place individually at the Northwest Conference XC Championships and led our team to our 6th straight conference team title. Then at the Regional Championships in Calaremont, CA I finished 15th individually and earned a individual bid to the national meet.
Photos: Christopher Sabato
 
Nationals was held at Principia College in Elsah, Illionis. It was a lot of fun, I did not race as well as I would have liked finishing 188th but I got some invaluable experience racing at this level.
Photos: Marissa Pariseau
 
I also recieved this at the end of the season because of my GPA and finish at the national meet.

Spring 2018

I did things a little bit differently this track season by running some more 10ks and 5ks. This was the first time I did not run the conference steeplechase, instead I ran the infamous 10k 5k double. After a hard fought 2nd place in the 10k I definitely felt it in the 5k but I'm still very happy with how it went.
