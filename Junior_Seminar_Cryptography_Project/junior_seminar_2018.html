Willamette University
 
This is the project that my group did in the Fall of 2017 for CS 389: Junior Seminar at Willamette University
This class is offered by the Willamette Computer Science department as a way for Juniors to get some experience with a independent project before working on a senior thesis. At the beginning of the semester we all split up into groups of about 2-3 people per group and we explored a new area of interest to us. The group I was in decided to Explore Cryptography. None of us knew anything about this field so we dove right in. See our final poster for a more in depth explanation of what we did and what we learned.
